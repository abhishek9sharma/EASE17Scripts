Racket-miniKanren
 Canonical miniKanren implementation in Racket. Asumu Takikawa and Sam Tobin-Hochstadt cleaned up William E. Byrd's Super Chobo Racket code, which in turn was based on the Scheme implementation of miniKanren in the paper: William E. Byrd, Eric Holk, and Daniel P. Friedman.
miniKanren, Live and Untagged: Quine Generation via Relational Interpreters (Programming Pearl).
