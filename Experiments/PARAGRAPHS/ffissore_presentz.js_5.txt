Rvl.io, Reveal.js, DZSlides and other hash/javascript/brower based slide systems that can fit into an IFrame
 Reveal.js, Rvl.io and DZSlides are some popular frameworks for creating very good looking HTML + CSS based slideshows. As they monitor the URL to know which slide has to be displayed, presentz.js supports them by wrapping the slideshow into an IFrame and changing its url.
